Our Cafe Journey

"It's fine to celebrate success
but it is more important to heed the
lessons of failure"


After loosing our loved ones to the pandemic
we understood how important it is to spent time
with our loved ones and to enjoy each and every moment of our life. The idea of bringing pepole
closer and enjoying every moment led to
Cup'O Joy. Originally it was established in 2021 ,
but due to the lockdown 2.0 the outlet were shut
down. But we didn't loose hope and decided to start again. The 2.0 journey of Cup'O Joy again started in March 2022.
And since then we are serving people and making memories.....
